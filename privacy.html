Chasing Majors Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2026

Chasing Majors builds sports card research tools, including the CM Rarity Gadget Chrome extension, Checklist Vault, Print Run Vault, release tools, and collector search experiences. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

When you use Chasing Majors tools, we may collect:

  • Account information, such as email address and subscription status.
  • Marketplace context, such as visible listing titles, search terms, marketplace source, and page URL from supported eBay and COMC pages when CM Rarity Gadget requests rarity intelligence.
  • Usage information, such as local free lookup counts, account activation status, and basic service logs needed to operate and troubleshoot the product.
  • Payment status, such as Stripe customer ID, subscription ID, and subscription state. Payment card details are handled by Stripe and are not stored by Chasing Majors.
  • Feedback you submit, including screenshots, page URLs, or descriptions you choose to send for support or data correction.

Chrome Extension Data

CM Rarity Gadget reads visible sports card listing and search text on supported eBay and COMC pages so it can request rarity intelligence from the Chasing Majors API and display the CM overlay. The extension stores your activation token, account status, and local free lookup count in Chrome local storage.

The extension does not collect payment card details, does not sell user data, and does not claim affiliation with eBay, COMC, Topps, Panini, Fanatics, or any marketplace or card manufacturer.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide rarity, scarcity, print run, and pack-odds context.
  • Verify account access and paid membership status.
  • Maintain daily free lookup behavior and account activation.
  • Improve data quality, matching accuracy, and product reliability.
  • Respond to support requests and fix reported errors.
  • Protect the service from abuse and diagnose technical issues.

Payments

Payments and subscription management are processed by Stripe. Chasing Majors receives subscription-related identifiers and status from Stripe so we can unlock paid features. We do not receive or store full card numbers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We share information only with service providers needed to operate Chasing Majors, such as hosting, database, analytics, support, and payment infrastructure, or when required by law.

Data Retention

We keep account and subscription records while your account is active or as needed for legitimate business, legal, tax, security, and troubleshooting purposes. Extension local storage can be cleared by removing the extension or clearing extension data in Chrome.

Your Choices

You can choose not to activate the extension, clear Chrome extension storage, cancel your subscription through Stripe billing, or contact Chasing Majors for account questions. Some features require account and subscription status to function.

Children

Chasing Majors is intended for sports card collectors and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

For privacy, support, or account questions, contact Chasing Majors at cm@chasingmajors.com.
